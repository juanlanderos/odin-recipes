Whipped Banana "Ice Cream"

Description

In this whipped ice cream recipe, frozen bananas have a nice refreshing ice cream-sorbet-type taste. It's a great way to satisfy your sweet tooth while also staying healthy.

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, peeled and sliced

Steps:

  • Place sliced bananas in an airtight container and freeze until solid, about 1 1/2 hours.
  • Blend frozen bananas in a blender until whipped and smooth.
  • You can add things like chocolate or peanute butter beforehand, for added flavor.
